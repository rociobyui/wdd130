Process to Have a Website

First, we need to schedule a meeting with you to learn about your business. Remember, your business is our priority. Second, we will meet to show you a professional wireframe of your website. Do not worry, a wireframe is like a draft of your website. We highly value your input! At this step, we can make the necessary changes we and you propose. Third, we will begin building your website, so you can leave this step to us! Fourth, you will see your website before putting it on the Internet. Minor changes can be made at this point! Finally, your website will be on the Internet.
